The University of Washington Invitational Taikai is held annually in the month of April. This tournament regularly draws
competitors from dojo throughout the Pacific Northwest Kendo Federation and Canada. The UW Taikai is often the a UW Kendo
Club member's first exposure to kendo tournaments. Now entering it's 26th year, this annual tradition draws more than 100
participants each year.



Tournament divisions are broken up as follows:
Mudansha 0-4 Kyu
Mudansha 3-1 Kyu
Yudansha 1-3 Dan
Yudansha 4 Dan and Above
